It Is Here ... And It Is Wonderful

Mar 7th, 2005

Well, the Tablet has arrived and here are my impressions with only 1 weekend under my belt.

  1. Wow … that’s light. My previous Sattelite from Toshiba now feels like a brick 
  2. Loving Ink. Alot. I’m someone who naturally does alot of hand notes and drawings. I’m officially out of the business of losing them from now on. 
  3. That said, you can’t replace the keyboard. It’s just to slow for alot of things. I find myself opening the tablet to laptop mode more than I fooled myself into believing I would prior to getting it.

Overall … love it!
